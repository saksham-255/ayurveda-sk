  1. WHO ARE WE?

Dr. Tayal’s ayurveda and Panchkarma centre is tradition of ayurvedic knowledge, Inherited in the scientific form as a complete Ayurvedic and Panchkarma centre. It has been established by Dr. Rajesh Tayal s/o Late Dr. D.k. Tayal who served society as Senior Ayurvedic Consultant retired as Deputy Director encouraging people towards Ayurveda.


Dr. Rajesh Tayal an Ayurveda & Panchkarma specialist has experience in the field of Ayurveda & Panchkarma for more than 26 years. He worked as Panchkarma Incharge and senior Ayurvedic Physician in Dabur Dhanwantary Hospital and Shri Dhanwantary college Chandigarh for 22yrs. He has also worked as Director & chief Consultant (Ayurveda) in Healthsure Multispecialty Hospital,Gharuan & as a Director & Head in the Department of Panchkarma in Saraswati Ayurveda Hospital and Medical College Mohali for 5 years. His passion about Ayurveda can never missed in any discussion with him. In his own words, ‘I don’t practice Ayurved, I love Ayurveda.


This centre is based on the deep knowledge and long term clinical practice and experience of our doctors as well as on the experience and careful training of our therapists. He take keen responsibility for establishing carefully considered and individualized treatments, as our goal is the ultimate health and well being of every patient who present. We do our best do deliver the highest quality work with the finest selection of herbal medication.
